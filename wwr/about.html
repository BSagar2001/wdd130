About Us - Exciting Expeditions

Our purpose is to bring adventure, teamwork, and unforgettable river experiences to every guest. Our mission is to explore the wild responsibly while creating memories that last a lifetime. Our creed is built on courage, respect for nature, and the spirit of the rapids. Our motto: “Ride Bold. Paddle Together.”

History

Founded in 1998 after a legendary wrong turn near the roaring “Flibber Falls,” RiverRush Rafting Co. began with two rafts, one soggy map, and a dream of adventure. Our purpose is to bring excitement, teamwork, and unforgettable river journeys to every guest who joins the ride. Our mission is to paddle boldly while respecting the wild spirit of the water and the stories it carries downstream. Guided by our creed of courage, camaraderie, and slightly questionable campfire coffee, we live by our motto: “Ride Bold. Paddle Together.”
